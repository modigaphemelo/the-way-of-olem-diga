Foundation Check

Before moving to Depth: do I have a stable base?

I have examined my operating system.
I have designed my physical environment.
I have audited my internal resources.
But can I sustain myself without external rescue?

The Four Requirements

1

Sensory Sanctuary

Do I have a physical environment I control?

Lighting, sound, temperature, and visual clutter are within my power to adjust. I have a space where I can reliably focus and recover.

2

Material Sovereignty

Do I have six months of expenses in accessible form?

Not in retirement accounts. Not in assets I cannot liquidate. Cash or equivalents that let me walk away without panic.

3

Skill Sovereignty

Do I have a skill that requires no credentials, only demonstrable output?

Something I can do that the market values, that doesn't require a license, degree, or permission. A portfolio, not a diploma.

4

Relational Sovereignty

Do I have at least one non-transactional relationship?

Someone who would show up if I had nothing to offer. Not a network contact. Not a mentor who benefits from me. Someone who walks with me.

The Counterfeit

!

Counterfeit Foundation

"I have isolated myself and called it sovereignty."

Withdrawal is not stability. Isolation without resources is just hiding. True foundation means I can engage the world from a position of strength, not retreat from it permanently.

The test: "Could I lose this and still walk? If my current arrangement collapsed tomorrow, would I have ground to stand on?"

The Decision

Before I proceed to Depth, I ask myself honestly:

  • Do I have a sensory environment I control?
  • Do I have six months of expenses saved?
  • Do I have a skill that doesn't need credentials?
  • Do I have at least one non-transactional relationship?

If I answered "no" to any of these: I remain in Foundation. This is not failure. This is accurate diagnosis. I cannot build depth on unstable ground.

If I answered "yes" to all: I am ready to face the shadow. Proceed to Depth.
